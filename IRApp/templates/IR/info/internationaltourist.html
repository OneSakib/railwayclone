The International Tourist Bureaus are located at various important cities all over India at the following locations :

KOLKATA CHENNAI BANGALORE AHMEDABAD
SECUNDERABAD JAIPUR JODHPUR VARANASI
NEW DELHI AGRA-CANTT. MUMBAI
New Delhi & Varanasi offices are known as International Tourist Bureau. International Tourist Bureau at New Delhi has started functioning round the clock w.e.f. 12-12-2014. These provide assistance to the foreign tourists and NRIs regarding enquiries, booking, reservation and travel planning etc.
